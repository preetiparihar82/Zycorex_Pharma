Corporate Profile

Quality and Consistency at the Heart of Healthcare

Zycorex Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a dedicated pharmaceutical company engaged in third-party manufacturing and marketing of premium medicine formulations. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we focus on distributing a wide array of life-supporting medicines to doctors, hospitals, clinical networks, and retail medical chemist outlets throughout the region.

As an ISO-certified company, quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction are at the very heart of our operations. We work in tandem with GMP-compliant contract manufacturers, allowing us to supply highly therapeutic, stable, and bio-available formulations at competitive pricing.
